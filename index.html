About the Game

Legends BMX is an extreme sports game where you can perform thrilling BMX tricks across various parks and scenarios. Embrace the adrenaline and flow of competing in exciting challenges, whether solo or online with friends.

Enjoy game modes ranging from Free Ride (no pressure, just pure fun) to competitive multiplayer with global leaderboards, all enhanced by Virtual Reality (VR) and Mixed Reality (XR) options for a fully immersive experience.

Key Features

  • Intuitive controls for a wide range of tricks and combos.
  • Multiple parks, ramps, and realistic environments for diverse playstyles.
  • VR and XR modes for an unparalleled immersive experience.
  • Global leaderboards and competitive online challenges.
  • Customize riders and bikes to stand out with a unique style.
