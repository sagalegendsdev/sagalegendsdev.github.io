Privacy Policy – Legends BMX

Legends BMX does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. The game is designed to be fully functional without requiring login, personal identification, or external account linking.

1. Data Collected

We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or location data.

Some non-personal, anonymized analytics data may be gathered by the platform (Meta Quest) for performance and crash reports. This data is handled by Meta, not by us.

2. Data Usage

No personal data is used, processed, or shared by the app.

Anonymous gameplay data, such as high scores or challenge completions, may be used to generate global leaderboards and improve game balance.

3. User Data Deletion

Since we do not store user-identifiable data, there is no data to request for deletion.

If you have concerns, you may contact us directly and we will assist you.

4. Data Protection & Responsibility

This application is developed and managed by the development team of Legends BMX VR.

We follow industry-standard best practices to ensure that any future features involving user data will comply with data protection laws such as GDPR and CCPA.

5. Contact

For any questions regarding privacy, please contact us at: contact@legendsbmxvr.com
