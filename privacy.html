Privacy Policy – Legends BMX

Legends BMX only stores the user's Meta display name for in-game features such as leaderboards and challenge tracking.

1. Data Collected

We collect the display name provided by the Meta platform. No other personal information such as email, location, or biometric data is collected.

2. Data Usage

The display name is used exclusively for displaying player names on global leaderboards and tracking progress in challenges. It is not shared with third parties.

3. User Data Deletion

If you wish to have your display name and related gameplay data removed from our servers, you can contact us and we will process your request promptly.

4. Data Protection & Responsibility

This application is developed and managed by the Saga Legends development team. We follow best practices to store and handle data securely, in compliance with regulations such as GDPR and CCPA.

5. Contact

For data removal requests or any privacy-related concerns, please contact us at: sagalegendsdev@gmail.com
